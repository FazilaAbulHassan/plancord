WHO WE ARE

About Infomaxis

Infomaxis Limited, Cork, Ireland is a brand committed to simplifying the works of employees and employers through efficient technologies. We’re the makers of the leading self-rostering app ideal for organizations and businesses that roster their staff.

Employee engagement is paramount for the growth of any organization. We’re a team that prioritizes employee engagement as well as their personal preferences and activities.

What is Plancord

Plancord Mobile App is the spark that ignites fair life-work balance among employees, as well as takes the hassle of managing rosters off the shoulder of the management of shift workers.Designed by nurses who have been through the arduous task of rostering, we realize the workload of creating duty rosters for employees while spending mundane hours and resources to make it happen. Plancord Mobile App automates everything you need about duty rosters for your brand. We make it easier for you to plan your daily life without hassle.
